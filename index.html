Preliminary Field Report

Welcome to the Redrock conservation zone. Our primary concern is the sudden and severe population collapse of the native Lesser Crested Fox. Initial surveys conducted between 2021 and 2025 indicate drastic disparities across the four major quadrants of the crater.

Biologists hypothesize that heavy metal seepage from an abandoned mine is contaminating the topsoil. For our upcoming breeding facility to be viable, the safety thresholds are strict: Lead (Pb) levels must remain below 15 mg/kg, and we have noticed severe behavioral anomalies when Mercury (Hg) levels exceed 4.0 mg/kg.
